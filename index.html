Biography

Greetings 🤗! I'm a graduate student at the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Seoul National University, working at AISDL (AI-driven Simulation and Design Lab) under the supervision of Prof. Do-Nyun Kim. Previously, I received my Bachelor's Degree from Seoul National University in Physics with a minor in Mechanical Engineering.

My research focuses on computational mechanics, AI-driven design, and simulation-based optimization. I develop advanced simulation and design methodologies that leverage artificial intelligence for engineering applications, with emphasis on topology optimization, multiscale modeling, and data-driven approaches for mechanical systems.

Currently, I am working at AISDL (AI-driven Simulation and Design Lab) , focusing on computational mechanics and AI-driven design methodologies.
